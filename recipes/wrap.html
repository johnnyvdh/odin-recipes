Wraps

Description

Easy meal of wraps filled with Oriental chicken in teriyaki sauce with
cucumber, mango and cashew nuts.

Ingredients

  • 300gr chicken breast
  • 1 head of romaine lettuce
  • 4 wraps
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 red onion
  • 4 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 125g mango
  • 4 tablespoons cashew nuts (unsalted)
  • oil or butter (for baking)

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breast into strips.

  2. Heat the oil or butter in a pan and add the chicken. Stir in the
    teriyaki sauce. Bake the chicken for 5 minutes until it is about
    cooked.

  3. Cut the red onion into rings. Add the red onion and bake for
    another minute.

  4. Cut the cucumber into thin strips or cut into strings with a spirelli
    cutter

  5. Divide 2 to 3 leaves of romaine lettuce over a wrap. Spoon a
    quarter of the chicken and some of the cucumber strings.Add
    some pieces of mango and cashew nuts and fold the wrap closed.
    Prick it with a prick. Repeat this with the rest of the ingredients.

  6. Tip: for a vegetarian dish, use pieces of vega chicken or tofu.
