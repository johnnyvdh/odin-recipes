Hash

Description

Delicious winter combination of soft stewed hash meat with red
cabbage and mashed potatoes with a crispy crusts from the oven.

Ingredients

  • 500gr red cabbage with apple (cooked)
  • 800gr mashed potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons breadcrumbs
  • Few lumps of butter

Hash:

  • 2 large onions
  • 40gr butter or margarine
  • 600gr hash meat / ribs
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 375ml beef stock
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3 cloves
  • pinch of paprika powder
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 bay leaves

Steps

  1. Cut the meat into pieces of about 3 cm. Let the butter or
    margarine melt in a (frying) pan. Add the meat and fry all around.
    Chop the onions and add to the meat.Season the meat with a
    pinch of paprika.Then add the flour and spoon through it.

  2. Add the red wine vinegar and then the sugar. then pour in the broth
    and stir well. Add the bay leaves and cloves (insert the cloves into
    the bay leaves so as not to lose them) and cook the hash 3
    hours until tender and soft with the lid on the pan. Remove the lid
    for the last 30 minutes to let the sauce thicken a bit. Meanwhile
    make the mashed potatoes. Also preheat the oven to 200 degrees.

  3. If the hash become too thick, you can add a little water to it.
    Remove the bay leaf and cloves. Divide the hash over the bottom
    of a baking dish. Divide the red cabbage with apple over this.
    Cover with a layer of mashed potatoes. Sprinkle the breadcrumbs
    over it and spread some lumps of butter over it. Put the baking
    dish in the oven for another 30 minutes.

  4. Tip: this recipe is also ideal for making a leftover hash.
