Lasagna

>

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 200 gr carrot
  • 125 gr celery
  • 500 gr ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon of oregano
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 small can of tomato puree (a 70 g)
  • 400 ml sieved tomatoes (tin)
  • 400 g diced tomatoes (tin)
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • 2 balls of mozzarella
  • 12 Lasagne sheets
  • handful of grated cheese

Steps

  1. preheat the oven to 200 degrees.

  2. Chop the onion and garlic. Brown this in a large pan with 1 tablespoon of oil.

  3. Cut the carrot and celery into small pieces and add to the pan.

  4. After a few minutes add the minced meat and fry.

  5. Add the oregano and thyme along with the tomato puree.

  6. Then add the tomato cubes and sieved tomatoes and crumble in
    the stock cube. Let the sauce simmer for about 30 minutes.

  7. If necessary, season the sauce with a pinch of salt and pepper.

  8. Meanwhile, make the bechamel sauce. Melt the butter in a pan
    and add the flour. Gently fry this roux for 3 minutes.

  9. Add the milk little by little and stir with a whisk until smooth. Bring
    the bechamel sauce to taste with a little salt and pepper and
    possibly nutmeg.

  10. Cut the mozzarella into slices.

  11. Grease the baking dish with a little oil or butter.

  12. Pour a little bit of the tomato sauce on the bottom. Put 3 lasagna
    sheets on top. Spoon a little tomato sauce on top and spread
    some b√©chamel sauce over it. Place a few slices of mozzarella on
    top. Cover with lasagna leaves and repeat until the ingredients are run
    out and finish with a layer of tomato sauce, bechamel sauce and
    mozzarella.

  13. Sprinkle the lasagna bolognese with the grated cheese and put in
    the oven for 45 minutes

  14. Tips: You do not have to pre-cook the lasagna sheets for this
    lasagna. If you do this, it only needs to be in the oven for about 25
    minutes.

  15. For a vegetarian variant, replace the minced beef with vegetarian
    minced meat.
